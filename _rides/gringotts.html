The Story

We start our adventure by entering the Gringotts lobby. We've been invited in as muggles to tour the vault. We walk past the goblins and down the stairs to a room where Gringotts employees take our pictures and point us in the right direction. As we wait, we can hear Harry, Ron and Hermione planning their break-in to Gringotts, although at this point, they don't know we're here. We're lead into a room where We go into a room where a goblin explains some safety rules for us and beckons us into an elevator that'll take us down to Gringotts. We descend into the depths of Gringotts banks and step out at the base of a large spiral staircase. After we grab our safety goggles, we climb to the top of the staircase and enter our vehicle. We pull into a large cavern and see Bill Weasley and his Gringotts escort ahead of us when suddenly the alarms go off and we're warned that there are imposters in Gringotts. Then to our right comes Bellatrix Lestrange. She tests if we're imposters by casting a spell that causes the track we're sitting on to tilt, leaving us hanging over an abyss. We plummet down and speed away only to come to a stop at the hands of Bill's spell. After he stops us we see Harry, Ron and Hermione speed by in their own cart after stealing a Horcrux. Security trolls reach up and grab our car, spinning it into the next room. As they wind up to hit us with their clubs, they trip and drag us down into the caves until we're stopped by Bill again. A dragon bursts through the cavern with Harry and company on its back. Bill sends us on our way as Gringotts security staff tries to stop the dragon. We enter the vault area where we see our frienly goblin beckoning us to safety. As we follow his instructions, a snake breaks through the wall and lunges at us until it is stopped by Voldemort and Bellatrix! He questions us and Bellatrix casts a spell on us as we go into the next room, a lava filled cavern. Voldemort appears and casts a fireball spell at us which nearly hits us until the dragon bursts through the cave and blows its own fire to knock the fireball out of the way. As Voldemort and Bellatrix flee, Harry hooks us to the dragon and our carts get pulled to safety. The last thing we see before we get back to the station is Bill telling us that Gringotts is still the safest place on Earth!
