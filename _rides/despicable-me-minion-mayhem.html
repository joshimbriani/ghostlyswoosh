Quick Facts

  • Housed in Soundstage 42.
  • Replaced Jimmy Neutron's Nicktoon Blast
  • Opened on July 2, 2012
  • The ride film won an Annie Award, the award for the animation industry.
  • It's no secret that Minions love bananas. To honor that, real banana trees are planted in front of the ride.

The Story

As we join the queue, we hear that we are entering Minion recruitment while hearing the story of Gru and his family. We then enter Gru's living room and receive our Minion Goggles. Gru and his daughters welcome us and tell us about the Minion training process and explain our Minion Goggles to us. We then enter Gru's lab where Gru and his Minions tell us how turning us into Minions is part of his plan to become the world's #1 super villian. Then Gru's girls enter the room and beg him to let them train us as Minions. He finally relents and walks out of the room, leaving the girls to supervise our training. We are then beckoned into the main ride chamber where we enter our vehicles. We get zapped with the Minion cannon and we slide down a slide which puts us on a track where we dodge various hazards like a cactus, lasers and fly swatter. We get punched by a giant boxing glove down into a chasm. We join the other Minions to make a Minion chain to get out of the chasm when we get distracted by a banana, the bridge falls apart and we fall down to the bomb making area of Gru's lab. A bomb dropping starts a chain reaction of other bombs exploding and we land on a gear rotating us around the factory. Gru saves the Minions ahead of us and grabs the girls flying vehicle until he gets knocked across the factory by a mallet and rides a cannonball stright into us, knocking us into the girls and them off of their ship. We end up on a conveyor belt headed into the anti-gravity room where we start floating. We float upwards and dodge closign metal vices until the anti gravity turns off and Gru comes to save the girls and us. Gru finally opens up the girl's present and shows them his gift to them, a huge amusement park and dance party. Gru congratulates us and a Minion accidentlly hits the Minion Ray, transforming us back into humans. We are let off into the gift shop where we can join a Minion dance party!
