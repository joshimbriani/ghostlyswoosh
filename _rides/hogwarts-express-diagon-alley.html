Quick Facts

  • Hogwarts is the first in park, park-to-park transportation ride.
  • You will need a park-to-park ticket to ride this ride.
  • The trains carry 30,000 passengers per day.
  • Passes through the Universal Orlando backlot.
  • Keep your eye out for the Platform 9 3/4 photo opportunity in Kings Cross Station.
  • Opened on July 8, 2014.

The Story

King's Cross to Hogsmeade
As we enter the station, we are taken past the iconic Platform 9 3/4 entrance that allows wizards to access to Hogwarts Express. We then reach the station where we are loaded into coaches. As we leave Kings Cross, we see Hedwig fly by and dementors creep over buildings. We hear Harry, Ron and Hermione pass by the doors of our coach. The lights then flicker and we see dementors pass by our coach until Harry uses a spell to drive them away. We then see Hagrid greet us and the Ford Anglia fly by. At this point, we arrive at Hogsmead and disembark.
Hogsmeade to King's Cross
As we leave Hogsmeade, we see Hagrid waving goodbye. We hear Harry, Ron and Hermione walk by our coach looking for a compartment. We see Buckbeak flying by and a beautiful vista of Hogwarts. Fred and George fly by telling us to visit their store while we're in Diagon Alley. We see Harry, Ron and Hermione walk by again with Harry grabbing a licorice cpider off of our door. We come up on the buildings of London where we watch the Knight Bus fit through impossible openings. Finally we pull into Kings Cross station and disembark.
