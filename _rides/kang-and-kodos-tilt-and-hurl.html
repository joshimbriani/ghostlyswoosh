The Story

As guests wait in line, they see clips from Treehouse of Horror episodes. When you board your spaceships, Kang & Kodos tell you that you've been duped and that the only way for them to let you go is to attack Springfield. As you ride, you hear various other Simpsons characters interacting with riders. As the ride ends, Kang & Kodos tells you that they had fun and that you should forget anything about this ride.
