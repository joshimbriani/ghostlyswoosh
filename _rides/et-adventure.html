The Story

In the queue, we can watch monitors showing us E.T. trivia and showing us filmmaker commentary about the movie. We enter the next room where Steven Spielberg comes on the screen telling us that E.T.'s home planet, the Green Planet, is dying and that it needs his healing touch to save it. We need to help him get there aboard flying bikes. We pick up our Interplanetary Passport and tell the cast member our name which gets programmed into the card. We enter the second queue which is a forest filled with a unique scent. We learn that the police are after E.T. and us. As we board our bikes, we ride through the forest and slip past NASA and the police who try to capture us. We almost run right into a police car until we leave the ground and start flying. We fly over a city where we can make out individual landmarks. We enter an intergalactic portal which takes us to E.T.'s planet, which is visibly dying. We meet his teacher Botanticus who tells us what we need to do to save it. E.T. uses his healing touch to bring the planet back to life and the planet's inhabitants start celebrating. We travel through this celebration until we pass by E.T. himself who thanks us by the name we gave earlier in the ride. We head back to Earth where we leave the bikes.
