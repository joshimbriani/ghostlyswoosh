Quick Facts

  • Set immediately after the first Shrek movie.
  • The posters outside the attraction are meant to satirize Disney.
  • Features the same voice talent as the movie.
  • Opened on June 12, 2003.

The Story

In the queue, we watch clips from the first Shrek movie and admire the posters satirizing popular Disney attractions. Then we are moved into the dungeon where we get our Ogre-Vision glasses. The Three Little Pigs, the Gingerbread Man and Pinocchio wake up in cages attached to the ceiling. They tell us teh story of how Lord Farquad captured them. The Magic Mirror wakes up and recaps the story (after digressing into a quick Star Wars style anecdote) of the first movie. The ghost of Farquad interrupts the story to demand that the Mirror tell him where Shrek and Fiona are. Farquad catches Pinocchio lying and orders his torturer Felonious to turn Pinocchio into firewood and the pigs into a luau. After questioning us, he orders us into his auditorium to be torutured. As we move into the toruture chamber, the curtains pull back and we see Shrek and Donkey trying to find a route to their honeymoon. Felonious runs up on a horse and kidnaps Felonious. Shrek and Donkey pursue in their carriage. They lose their carriage after a jump into the graveyard and start searching for Fiona there. Farquad's ghost animates a giant stone dragon who chases them until Donkey calls his own dragon. On the back of the dragon, they catch up to Fiona only to be chased by the stone dragon. Diving into a trench, they mimic the Death Star trench run from STar Wars, eventually causing the pursuing stone dragon to lose its wings. Farquad and Felonious float down a river getting closer and closer to a waterfall. Farquad reveals that he means to let Fiona die so that he can be with her ghost. Right as she's about to fall down the waterfall, Shrek and Donkey show up and catch Fiona on their dragon. The dragon shoots a fireball at Farquad and he disappears. The movie then cuts to the Hotel Honeymoon where Shrek and Fiona are happily spending their honeymoon.
