The Story

We enter the queue to a NEST facility under seige with alarms blaring all around us. Through video briefings, we learn that Decepticons are attacking the facility looking for the AllSpark. We hear from Optimus Prime who tells us taht our mission is to accompany Evac in getting the AllSpark out of the facility. We pick up our safety goggles and get ready for our adventure. After we board Evac, we immediately run into Ravage who grabs the AllSpark from us. Turning around, we see Bumblebee fighting Sideways and getting us the AllSpark back. We start to escape but encounter Megatron fighting Optimus Prime. Megatron grabs us until we're able to break away after the water pipe he's holding onto breaks off. We look for a way out only to turn around to Megatron shooting a missile at us. We manage to get out of the building through the hole created by the explosion. As soon as we get outside Devastator immediately starts suctioning up everything in our path but we're able to reverse and get away. After we get some assistance from Sideswipe getting past Bonecrusher, we run into Devastator again. But instead of running this time, we engage our shields and go right through him. As soon as we get free of Devastator Starscream grabs us and throws us across the city. We run across Optimus fighting Megatron again with Optimus starting to lose. Instead of running this time, we jam the AllSpark in Megatron's chest, causing him to overload. Bumblebee catches us from our fall and Optimus thanks us for our help. We then disembark and enter the Transformers Supply Vault gift shop.
