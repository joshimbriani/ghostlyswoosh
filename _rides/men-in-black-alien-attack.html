Quick Facts

  • Set at the 1964 World's Fair.
  • Replaced a Back to the Future III locomotive display.
  • The ride's fog machines uses 70,000 gallons of liquid nitrogen every day.
  • If you shake the handles on some of the doors in the queue, you might hear an alien's message for you!
  • If you look at the windows as you ride through the New York City scene in the ride, you'll see storefronts for "I.M. Hotep Jewelry Store" and "Flaming Moe's Bar" which are references to Revenge of the Mummy and The Simpsons respectively.
  • If you look right before you enter the scanners, you'll see an alien holding up a newspaper. When he drops it, you'll see that it's Steven Spielberg himself.
  • On the far right side of the red side of the ride, you can see Frank the Pug in the newspaper stand. Hit him for extra points!
  • During quiet times, ask a cast member for an Immigration Tour and you can go behind the scenes of the ride!
  • There are two different endings for the ride depending on your car's score.
  • The max score available is 999,999.
  • Opened on April 14, 2000.

The Story

The posters and decorations in line point to a World's Fair exhibit named The Universe and You. We enter into a room full of stained glass and an attendent starts a narration that introduces us to our Universe and You exhibit. Until suddenly the narration comes to a halt and a voice welcomes us recruits to the Men In Black training facility. The wall opens and an MIB agent tells us to get into teh elevator. After we get out of the elevator, we queue past various MIB aliens and props. We then enter the immigration room where aliens from the ride oversee the various extraterrestrial activities with alarms going off every so often when aliens threaten to blow up Earth. Eventually we make our way to the main part of the building where we board our vehicles and start off on our training. We enter the training room and blast the targets until MIB Director Zedd tells us that a prison transport ship crashed in NYC and he needs our help to round them up. We go through a New York street packed full of aliens. We go into an alley and get ambushed by an alien and spin out of control. We then go through a scanner facing the other track's cars and find out that they're really aliens. If we hit their fusion exhaust port over their ride vehicle they wpin out of control, but so do we if they hit ours. Finally, Agent J comes on the TVs at Times Square anf tells us that there's one massive alien that escaped. As we round the corner we can see him but our guns have no effect. We enter his mouth only to be told to push our red button and deploy our Subatmoic Thermonuclear Disruptor which defeats the alien. J comes on the screen again at this point and congratulates whichever side had more points. We see whichever ending of the ride our car earned and then are neuralyzed so we don't remember anything of it. We pull back into the station to a huge sign telling us that of course we're alone in the universe!
