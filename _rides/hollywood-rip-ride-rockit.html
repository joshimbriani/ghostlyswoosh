Quick Facts

  • Travels into Citywalk
  • Currently the tallest coaster at the Universal Orlando resort.
  • Replaced a boneyard full of props and past attractions.
  • Has a secret song list, to access press and hold the ride logo on the touchpad for a few seconds and enter the code that corresponds to the song you want.
